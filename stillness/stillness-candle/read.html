A Candle Meditation

One. Choose a quiet and comfortable place to sit.
You can sit on the floor or on a chair, as long as your
back is straight and your feet are firmly planted on the ground.

Two. Place a candle in front of you, at eye level, about an
armâ€™s length away.

Three. Light it and gaze without blinking your eyes.
Try to keep your eyes focused on it and avoid any distractions.

Four. If your eyes start to water, take a break and
blink a few times. Then resume your focus.

Five. If your mind wanders, gently bring your attention back.

Six. Continue to gazing for as long as you feel comfortable.
Start with just a few minutes and gradually increase the time
as you become more comfortable with the practice. Work
yourself up to fifteen-twenty minutes.

Seven. When you are ready to finish, close your eyes and focus
on your breath for a few minutes. Take deep breaths in and out,
and try to relax your mind and body.
