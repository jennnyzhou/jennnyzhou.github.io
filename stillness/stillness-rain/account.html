October First


Today I went to do a proper grocery run for the first
time since I moved into my new apartment (a month
ago). I feel like because the moving process had been
so overwhelming, I had put off doing groceries and
actually cooking, which then meant I was eating out
and ordering delivery everyday. I feel like for the past
few years, I often found cooking was a way in which I
was able to nourish my body. Because some family was
also visiting, eating out everyday definitely made me
feel gross. But today has been the first official step to
get back on this routine, which also means a little
break for my wallet.
